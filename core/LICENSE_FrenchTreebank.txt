The French version of ReVerb uses models for processing several common Natural 
Language Processing tasks in French with Apache OpenNLP. These models are 
made available by Nicolas Hernandez here:

https://sites.google.com/site/nicolashernandez/resources/opennlp

Their licence states

The French Treebank licence does not prevent to distribute its analysis 
results under whatever licence but it mentions that the it should be used only 
for research purpose. Consequently we restrict the use of these models only 
for research purposes.

NO COMMERCIAL USE.
